About
Noodles Etc is a Thai restaurant in the heart of Chicago's UIC campus. They offer large portions and reasonable prices, making it a great option for budget-minded students. Although their Thai food isn't particularly amazing, it's still good enough to satisfy most appetites.
Why you should go
1
Enjoy delicious curry options with customizable spice levels
2
Try standout dishes like croquettes and Bai Mi sandwich
3
Experience a calm, clean, and inviting atmosphere
Reviews
Noodles Etc offers a mixed experience with great tasting food but inconsistent service. Reviewers praise the delicious curry options and customizable spice levels, highlighting dishes like croquettes and Bai Mi sandwich as standout choices. While some find the flavors lacking in certain dishes, others appreciate the generous portion sizes and friendly staff. The atmosphere is described as calm, clean, and inviting despite occasional issues with service efficiency.
